Chords and Chord Qualities

A chord is when you play three or more musical notes at the same time. Depending on quality of the chords, they can sound happy, sad, tense, or relaxed.

Type Associated Sound
Major Happy or bright
Minor Sad or serious, melancholic
Diminished Tense or spooky
Augmented Unusual or dreamy
In this tutorial, we will mainly discuss major and minor chords.
